MR NGOBESE:: Yes, I know it is Shell House.

MR NGOBESE:: Yes, I can.

MR P A VAN WYK: Please proceed.

MR P A VAN WYK: Were you lying down when you were shot at?

MR NGOBESE:: Yes, I was lying down.

MR NGOBESE:: I was injured on my knee, on my right knee. I would like to show you. Let me do this so you can see exactly. This is where I got injured, maybe the ones who are right next to me are able to see exactly. The bullets were extracted but some liquid was drawn from the knee.

MR P A VAN WYK: After you were shot, did you attend the meeting at Library Gardens?
