NONKWANDA NORA MAYIDUME: (sworn states)

CHAIRPERSON: Nonkwanda Nora you have heard the evidence that was put before the Commission. We have a statement before us on your behalf. Is there something that you would like to add?

MS MAYIDUMA: Thank you. There is nothing that I would like to add.

CHAIRPERSON: You, therefore, confirm that this evidence that was put before us is true?

You are quite young. How are you involved in this incident?

MS MAYIDUMA: I was three months old when this incident occurred. My brother told me what happened.

CHAIRPERSON: Thank you madam.
