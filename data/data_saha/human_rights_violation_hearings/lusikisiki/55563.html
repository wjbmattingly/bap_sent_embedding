NORMA KUBISHI MBWANA: (sworn states)

CHAIRPERSON: You have heard Thlapo Silangwe giving evidence here yesterday. Is there something that you would like to add?

MS MBWANA: No Sir. I am not going to add anything.

I am very glad that Nonqulana was mentioned here today and it was clarified that Nonqulana is a historical mountain, so that the next generations know that this was the origin where people died for the land.

If the Government can do something about Nonqulana we would be very pleased.

Thank you.
