Dick's friend does not say anything about the accident. It is a policeman who tell us about the incident. So we were told on the Wednesday, at 12 midnight when he was at his fiancee's home in Ladysmith and then the following morning Dick was supposed to go to Welkom for a - to go and investigat a case, which up to this day we are not sure what the case was about.
